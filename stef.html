Stef Animal

Stef is an experimental multi-instrumentalist, solo artist, and game designer. She sings in shoegaze ensemble The Golden Awesome, with whom she has toured internationally. Her solo work is defined by a "gadget-pop" aesthetic, most notably on the 2018 album Top Gear, which was composed entirely using obsolete 1980s computers and home keyboards. Alongside her musical output, she works extensively in interactive media, frequently exploring the intersection of nostalgic lo-fi textures and sophisticated digital soundscapes.
