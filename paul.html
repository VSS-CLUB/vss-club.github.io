Paul Fox

Paul is an experimental musician and audio professional based in Dunedin, New Zealand. A figure in the international space-rock scene, he has been a member of Nik Turner's Space Ritual, Farflung, Pressurehed, and Anubian Lights. He is also one half of the duo The Brain with Tommy Greñas, with whom he toured North America supporting Cluster. His earlier history includes tours and recordings with Trash Can School and collaborations with Helios Creed and Chrome. Alongside his ongoing musical work, he applies decades of analogue expertise to sound design for film, game audio, and interactive media.
